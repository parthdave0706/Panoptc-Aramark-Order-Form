Theft Detection Plans

Bronze Annual is the baseline tier applied to every location by default. The recommendations on each plan below identify the situations where a higher tier is warranted, typically driven by monthly losses, market volume, new market status, or known theft activity.

Diamond Plan

$150 / Month / Location

Analysis of suspicious kiosk behavior AND when users exit market without paying.

4 weekly reviews (Theft reports every week).

Recommended for New, High volume markets, locations with $500+ in monthly losses, high theft markets, and any account where tracking repeat offenders or grab and go theft is a priority. Best fit for problematic locations.

Gold Plan

$90 / Month / Location

Analysis of suspicious kiosk behavior.

2 weekly reviews (Theft reports every week).

Recommended for Locations with $300+ in monthly losses, any location currently being billed for theft with responsive clients who want regular footage delivery to address it. Best fit for average to slightly above average theft levels.

Gold Annual Plan

$999 / Year / Location

Analysis of suspicious kiosk behavior.

2 weekly reviews (Theft reports every week).

Recommended for Same coverage as Gold Plan with annual billing. Best fit for locations with sustained or recurring theft patterns where weekly review is expected to be needed long term.

Silver Plan

$65 / Month / Location

Analysis of suspicious kiosk behavior.

2 reviews every 2 weeks (Theft reports twice a month).
